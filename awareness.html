Please take 5 minutes of your time to
watch this short informational video regarding
mental health and the importance of
your well-being.

Acknowledging and addressing your
emotional needs is just as important as
treating your physical needs. This video is one
of the many that address mental health
head on, and there are many others similar
to this one provided to you
on multiple platforms.

*.゜｡:+.゜｡*

There are many different ways to
approach mental health, and a small webpage
such as mine will never be enough
to cover it all.

If you ever find yourself struggling with
depression, anxiety, truama, mental disorders,
physical disorders, or all of the above-
there is help.

In the navigation bar above,
I have provided numerous helpful sources
that could open you up to yourself, or
may help you in some way.

Please reach out for a hand if you need one.
